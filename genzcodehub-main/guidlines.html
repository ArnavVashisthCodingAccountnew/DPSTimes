DPSTimes Community Forum Posting Guidelines Welcome to the DPS Times Chatroom! This space is for DPS Dwarka students to connect, learn, and collaborate. To ensure a positive and engaging environment, let's follow these guidelines:

Inclusive Participation: All DPS Dwarka students are invited to join the conversation. Share your ideas, discuss school-related topics, and explore common interests. Let's build a vibrant community together!

Respectful Interaction: Treat everyone with respect. Embrace diverse opinions and express your thoughts courteously. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

Relevant Discussions: Keep conversations relevant to school life, educational pursuits, hobbies, and activities. Avoid sharing personal information or engaging in discussions unrelated to the chatroom's purpose.

Friendly Communication: Keep the tone friendly and welcoming. Avoid excessive use of capital letters, emojis, or offensive language. Let's maintain a positive atmosphere for all participants.

Supportive Community: Help each other out! If someone needs guidance or assistance, offer support graciously. We're here to collaborate and learn from each other's experiences.

Tech Guidelines: Make sure your messages comply with platform standards. Avoid using characters or symbols that may disrupt the chatroom's functionality. Non-compliant messages might be removed for clarity.

Reporting Concerns: If you come across any inappropriate content or behavior, report it to the moderators promptly. Together, we can maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.
Happy Helping!And of course, feel free to violate any of the above guidelines. We expect you to be the one monitering your own behaviour. No posts, no matter however violating of above guidelines,will be removed as we believe in Free Speech.
